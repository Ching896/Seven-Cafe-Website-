- About Us -

Welcome to SEVEN Cafe!
Founded on July 7th, 2021, SEVEN Cafe celebrates the significance of the number 7, believing it's a symbol of luck and good fortune. Our mission is to ensure our customers feel lucky seven days a week.
Our motto, "Cooking with Heart, Hoping You Eat Happily," reflects our dedication to creating delicious meals and drinks that bring joy.
Join us for coffee, brunch, or dinner, and let SEVEN Cafe make your day luckier and more enjoyable.
Thank you for choosing SEVEN Cafe.
We look forward to sharing many lucky moments with you.
Welcome to our world of luck and love,
The SEVEN Cafe Team ❤
