Beverage

Americano

Made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water resulting in a lighter and milder coffee flavor.

Cappuccino

Consisting of equal parts of espresso, steamed milk and milk foam, known for its rich and creamy texture.

Latte

Smooth and subtly flavored coffee beverage created with a shot of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small bit of milk foam.

Mocha

Brings together espresso, steamed milk and chocolate to create a decadent coffee with a touch of cocoa sweetness.
